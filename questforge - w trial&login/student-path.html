The Student Journey

A visual walkthrough of the student's progression system, from onboarding to daily quest management.

Step 1: Class Selection

Choosing the 'Student' path initializes the gamified curriculum.

Step 2: Onboarding

Students enter the forge through a secure gateway to save progress.

Step 2: Central Dashboard

Students access their personalized dashboard to track progress and quests.

Step 3: Identity Setup

Defining character traits, avatars, and monitoring rank progression.

Step 4: Profile Set Up

The student's identity, ensuring a personalized experience.

Step 5: Quest Hub

The central destination where students manage teacher-assigned missions.

Step 6: The Boss Battle

Students face off against challenging bosses to test their skills.

Step 7: Boss Battle Lives

If student the answer is wrong, they lose number of lives.

Step 8: Boss Battle's HP

If student answers correctly, the boss will lose HP

Step 9: Boss Battles Hint

Students can use hints to gain an advantage in battles.

Step 10: Boss Battles Reward

Students earn rewards for defeating bosses, enhancing their skills.

Step 11: Achievements

The central destination where students manage their achievements and progress.
