The Teacher's Journey

A visual walkthrough of the teacher's progression system, from onboarding to daily quest management.

Step 1: Class Selection

Choosing the 'Teacher' path initializes the gamified curriculum.

Step 2: Onboarding

Teachers enter the forge through a secure gateway to save progress.

Step 2: Central Dashboard

Teachers access their personalized dashboard to track/manage progress and quests.

Step 3: Identity Setup

Defining character traits, avatars, and monitoring rank progression.

Step 4: Profile Set Up

The teacher's identity, ensuring a personalized experience.

Step 5: Quest Record

The central destination where teachers manage student-assigned missions.

Step 6: Class Progress

The hub for tracking student progress and quest completion.

Step 7: Deploy Boss Battle

The climax of the teacher's journey, where they deploy a challenging boss battle for their students.
