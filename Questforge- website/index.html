Leaderboard

The Leaderboard showcases the top-performing students within the QuestForge community. Rise through the ranks by completing high-difficulty bounties, maintaining your study streaks, and earning legendary badges. Only the most dedicated students will see their names cemented at the top of the realm. The most disciplined and persistent students—those who treat every challenge as an opportunity for growth—will see their names permanently cemented at the pinnacle of the realm, inspiring the next generation of scholars to begin their own quest for excellence.

Forged to empower the entire realm.

QuestForge unites Students and Teachers, turning every challenge into a collective victory for the realm.

For Teachers
  • Create quests with custom difficulty levels and XP rewards.
  • Award special badges and titles for exceptional student performance.
  • Track class-wide progress and identify students who need support.
  • Review submissions to trigger level-ups and reward student effort.
  • Launch boss battles - large-scale challenges for the entire class to conquer.
For Students
  • Turn your assignments into missions and complete them to earn XP.
  • Watch your character grow as you master subjects and increase your rank.
  • Unlock rare achievements for consistency, speed, and academic effort.
  • Compete on the leaderboard to become the realm's top student.
  • Monitor your progress and stats through your personal hero dashboard.

Forged with Purpose.

QuestForge isn't just a platform; it's a movement to redefine how we approach education through gamification.

Our Mission
  • To bridge the gap between academic rigor and student engagement.
  • To empower educators with tools that celebrate progress and growth.
  • To foster a collaborative environment where every student feels like a hero.
  • To encourage consistency by rewarding discipline and academic effort.
Our Vision
  • To become the global standard for gamified learning ecosystems.
  • To transform classrooms into realms of infinite possibility and discovery.
  • To inspire a lifelong love for learning by making effort visible and rewarding.
  • To build a future where education is an adventure, not a chore.
