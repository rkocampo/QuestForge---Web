Transform Your Academic Journey into a Game.

QuestForge is a desktop productivity application that revolutionizes how you manage your studies. Turn mundane assignments into exciting "Quests", earn XP, level up, and conquer your semester!

📜 Quests

Turn your assignments into missions. Complete them to earn XP and level up your character.

🎖️ Achievements

Earn special badges like Early Bird or Task Master by hitting your study goals and staying consistent.

👤 Profile

Your personal hero dashboard. Track your current level, total XP, and overall rank at a glance.

👤 Your Profile Hub

The Profile Hub serves as a central dashboard for tracking your personal evolution throughout your academic journey. It provides a real-time summary of your character’s growth, displaying current statistics, unlocked achievements, and a history of recent activities to ensure you always know your standing. By monitoring your progress and milestones in one place, you can stay motivated as you level up and master your studies.

🏹 Our Mission

To equip students with the ultimate gamified task-management weapon. By transforming assignments into bounties and exams into boss battles, QuestForge motivates learners to conquer their daily quests, level up their skills, and claim their rightful achievements in a collaborative, engaging environment.

Whether you are fighting solo to climb the Individual Leaderboards or teaming up with your guildmates to conquer Group Projects, your academic journey is now completely in your hands. Level up, earn badges, and prove your knowledge!
