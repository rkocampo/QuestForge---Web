Data Protection Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect your Hero Name (Username), Email, and academic progress (XP, Levels, and Badges) to provide a personalized gamified experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for leaderboard rankings, tracking assignment completions, and notifying you of new Quests or Boss Battles issued by your Teachers.

3. Security of Your Scrolls

We use industry-standard encryption to ensure that your passwords and academic records are guarded against dark wizards and unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Sharing

QuestForge does not sell your data to external merchants. Your progress is only visible to your designated Teachers and fellow students on the Leaderboard.

5. Your Rights

You may request to delete your character and all associated data at any time by contacting our Citadel support team.
